Apple Newton eMate 300

Date of Aquisition - Late 2000's, around 2007-2008?


This is the "laptop" model from Apple's failed PDA product line, the Newton.

I bought it from eBay, cannot find the original purchase email, but I probably spent $25 or $30 on it. From what I remember, it was a school was clearing them out.

Current specs are:
  • 25 MHz ARM 710a RISC CPU
  • 3MB RAM
  • Dual Double-sided 390k 5.25 Floppy Drives
  • 6.8" 480x320 resolution 16-shade grayscale display
  • Serial Number: IV7272LP8TD
